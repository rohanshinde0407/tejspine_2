Pain Conditions We Support

Back Pain

Support for lower, middle, and upper back discomfort caused by posture strain or daily activity.

Neck Pain

Care for stiffness, tightness, and movement difficulty related to neck pain.

Shoulder Pain

Relief for shoulder pain caused by muscle tightness or work-related stress.

Thoracic Pain

Support for thoracic pain affecting the middle back and posture balance.

Slip Disc

Non-surgical support for slip disc to improve comfort and movement.

Sciatica

Knee Pain

Support programs for knee pain related to stiffness, joint stress, or daily use.
